Privacy Policy

Loyman Automotive respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect through this website and how we use it.

1. What we collect

When you submit an enquiry or feedback form, we collect the details you provide — typically your name, phone number, email, vehicle details and your message. We don't collect more than we need to help you.

2. How we use it

We use your information only to respond to your enquiry, book and carry out work, and follow up about your vehicle. We don't sell or rent your information to anyone.

3. Form submissions

Our website forms are delivered to us by a third-party form service (FormSubmit) so messages reach our inbox. Your details are transmitted to us by email and handled according to this policy.

4. Storage & security

We keep your information only as long as needed for the purpose it was provided and take reasonable steps to protect it.

5. Your rights

Under the Privacy Act 2020 you can ask to see the personal information we hold about you and request corrections. Contact us using the details below.

6. Contact
