What we do

Servicing and repairs for every vehicle

Whatever your car needs, we keep it running safely and reliably. Here's the work that comes through the workshop most.

01

WOF Inspections

Warrant of Fitness checks done thoroughly. If something fails, we tell you exactly why and what it'll take to pass.

02

General Servicing

Oil and filter changes, fluids, belts and a full safety check to keep your car healthy and reliable.

03

Brakes & Suspension

Pads, rotors, shocks and struts. We sort that grinding, pulling or bouncing so the car stops and handles like it should.

04

Engine Diagnostics

Warning light on? We plug in, read the fault codes and track down the real cause instead of guessing.

05

Tyres & Alignment

New tyres, rotations and wheel alignment to stop uneven wear and keep the car tracking straight.

06

Batteries & Auto Electrical

Flat batteries, dodgy starting and electrical gremlins tested and fixed so the car turns over every time.
